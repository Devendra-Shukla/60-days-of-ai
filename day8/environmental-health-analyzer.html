  • ✓ Schedule important outdoor activities for early mornings (better air quality)
  • ✓ Monitor ozone alerts during summer months (June-August)

🏠 Indoor Air Quality Improvements

  • ✓ Install HEPA air purifier in bedroom (especially important with current AQI)
  • ✓ Use MERV-13 or higher furnace filters, replace monthly
  • ✓ Keep windows closed on high AQI days (AQI > 50)
  • ✓ Run air purifier 8+ hours daily during high pollution periods
  • ✓ Add indoor plants: Snake plants, Pothos, Spider plants improve air naturally
  • ✓ Test for radon in basement (DFW region consideration)

🏃 Outdoor Activity Guidance

  • ✓ AQI 0-50 (Good): No restrictions, enjoy all outdoor activities
  • ✓ AQI 51-100 (Moderate): Sensitive groups should limit prolonged exertion
  • ✓ AQI >100 (Poor): Avoid strenuous outdoor activity, stay indoors
  • ✓ Best times: Early morning (5-10 AM) has lowest pollution levels
  • ✓ Exercise in parks away from highways/heavy traffic areas

💇 Hair Care Recommendations

  • ✓ Use clarifying shampoo 1-2x weekly to remove hard water buildup
  • ✓ Install shower filter to reduce mineral content from hard water
  • ✓ Apply deep conditioning treatment 2x weekly (hard water increases dryness)
  • ✓ Use chelating shampoo monthly to strip mineral buildup
  • ✓ Air dry or use cool setting to reduce damage from dry conditions
  • ✓ Consider reverse osmosis filtration system for whole-house water softening

🧴 Skin Care Recommendations

  • ✓ Use daily SPF 30+ sunscreen (UV + pollution protection)
  • ✓ Apply antioxidant serum (Vitamin C) to neutralize pollution particles
  • ✓ Double cleanse at night to remove PM2.5 particles (oil cleanser + water cleanser)
  • ✓ Moisturize 2x daily due to hard water mineral deposits on skin
  • ✓ Use gentle, sulfate-free cleansers (avoid harsh soaps with hard water)
  • ✓ Apply niacinamide serum to strengthen skin barrier against pollution

💧 Water Quality Improvements

  • ✓ Install point-of-use water softener for kitchen sink (for cooking/drinking)
  • ✓ Use reverse osmosis filter for drinking water (removes additional contaminants)
  • ✓ Install whole-house softener (protects all appliances, improves skin/hair)
  • ✓ Replace water filters every 3-6 months depending on usage
  • ✓ Run water for 30 seconds after extended non-use (reduces lead exposure)
  • ✓ Use bottled water for cooking if concerned about tap water hardness
